Welcome to the official website of Holy Ghost Revival Mission Student Fellowship, Obafemi Awolowo University Chapter. We are a group of students who love God and love people. We believe in the power of the Holy Spirit to transform lives and communities. We invite you to join us in our fellowship, worship, and service activities.

Our Vision

To be a dynamic and faith-filled community of students, united in our pursuit of a deeper relationship with God and each other. We envision a vibrant student church that equips and empowers young adults to navigate the challenges of college life with unwavering faith, fosters genuine friendships, and inspires them to impact their campus and the world through love, service, and the transformative message of Christ.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a welcoming and empowering community for students, where we cultivate and strengthen their faith, provide a supportive network of peers, and encourage active engagement in the life of our campus. Through meaningful worship, discipleship, and service, we strive to equip young adults to navigate the challenges of university life, live out their Christian values, and be a positive influence on their campus and beyond.
